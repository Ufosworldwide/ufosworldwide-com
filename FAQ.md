## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I use Presignal research in my own work?**  
Yes. All preprints are published under CC BY 4.0. Credit John Ernest 
Carter and cite the specific DOI of the work you are referencing.

**Can I contribute to this repository?**  
Yes. Open an issue describing your proposed contribution before 
submitting a pull request. This keeps contributions aligned with 
active research directions.

**Can I fork this repository?**  
Yes, under CC BY 4.0 terms with full attribution.

**What counts as a scientific integrity violation?**  
Misrepresenting epistemic status, stripping attribution, fabricating 
citations, or representing preprint findings as peer-reviewed conclusions.

**How long does it take to get a response to a report?**  
Acknowledgement within 7 days. Resolution timeline depends on 
complexity.

**I have a research collaboration proposal — where do I send it?**  
jubecrew@gmail.com with subject line `[COLLABORATION]`

**I found a bug or error in a published preprint — what do I do?**  
Open an issue in this repository with the DOI, the specific error, 
and your suggested correction. All corrections are reviewed seriously.
