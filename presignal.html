Water Science · 2026
Seasonal Source Water Temperature as a Predictive Indicator for Legionella Outbreak Risk
First correlation analysis connecting Ontario source water temperature to confirmed Legionella case counts. Demonstrates a 2–6 week predictive window before outbreak probability rises — a signal that has been in the public data for 60 years.
Submitted to PHO · OHA zenodo.19695022
Water Science · 2026
Chronic Source Water Quality Exceedances in the Grand River Watershed
Analysis of 7,630,482 measurements across 438 Ontario stations. 97.6% of Grand River stations chronically exceed provincial standards. Direct implications for AI data center cooling infrastructure in Cambridge and Brantford.
48 views · 27 downloads zenodo.19653415
Earth Science · 2026
The Geological Pathway Diversity Model (GPDM) v2.4
Six-pathway unified classification framework for anomalous luminous phenomena. Built on 30+ peer-reviewed citations spanning NASA, USGS, QuakeFinder, and Project Hessdalen. Championed by Fred Pallesen, CEO Project Hessdalen.
317 views · 25 downloads EarthArXiv 12638
Space Science · 2026
The Traveling System: Galactic Forcing of Terrestrial Climate Through Heliospheric Variability
Proposes that solar system galactic motion — traversing energetically distinct ISM regions — modulates the heliosphere and drives climate forcing at timescales inaccessible to orbital or anthropogenic models alone.
106 views · 18 downloads zenodo.19717287
Space Science · 2026
The Interstellar Medium as Navigable Terrain
Reframes the ISM from obstacle to navigable terrain. Identifies the Beta CMa tunnel as a candidate low-resistance departure corridor. Proposes a dedicated Interstellar Terrain Mapping mission as Phase 1 precursor to optimized interstellar departure.
59 views · 17 downloads zenodo.19717522
Medical · 2026
The Immunological Tinnitus Subtype (ITS): RA, TNF-α, and Autoimmune-Targeted Treatment
Proposes a formally defined disease category linking rheumatoid arthritis to tinnitus through three primary mechanisms including TNF-α neuroinflammation. Synthesizes 28 peer-reviewed sources across five medical disciplines.
82 views · 50 downloads zenodo.19654306

Presignal · The Mission

Every paper Presignal has published started with a question nobody was asking. Why does Legionella spike every June in Ontario when the signal is readable six weeks earlier? Why does profit flatline when revenue climbs? Why does the solar system's position in the galaxy not appear in climate models?

The answers were always there. They required someone willing to look at the data without an institutional agenda telling them what they were allowed to find.

That is what Presignal does. One problem at a time.
