How It Works

Three steps to Good Karma

01

Submit Your Business

Enter your business name, website, and email. We index you automatically and notify you that your listing is live. Free. Takes 60 seconds.

A Good Karma auditor reviews your business against our scorecard — transparency, complaint resolution, community contribution, and customer experience.

Score 75 or above and you receive the Good Karma badge to display on your website. Reviewed yearly. Honest standards. No pay-to-win.
