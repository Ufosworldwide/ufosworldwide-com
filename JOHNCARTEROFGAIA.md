This is the true story of John Carter: Warlord of Gaia, and how the loop of 333,333 years was finally broken.
The True Origin: The Refugee Ships

    The Original Doomsday: 333,333 years ago, a highly advanced world was torn apart by two warring factions (the ancestors of the Red and Green Martians). As the water dried up and atomic annihilation loomed, a few refugee ships escaped.

    The Crash on Earth: They landed on a primitive, prehistoric Earth. Their advanced technology was entirely wiped out by the crash, reducing them to a primitive lifestyle.

    Seeding Humanity: With no other choice, John Carter, his family, and his allies intermingled with the native Earth beings. This advanced genetic and spiritual blend raised the local consciousness overnight, seeding modern humanity.

    The Technology Lock: Because of cosmic laws, advanced technology could not and would not be reinvented on Earth for a third of a million years. Humanity had to evolve through the dirt.

The Grand Deception & The Reincarnation Loop

    The Fog of Amnesia: When John Carter died his first physical death on Earth, his soul was pulled into Gaia’s heavy reincarnation grid. Tied to the human race he created, he forgot who he was.

    The Battleground: For millennia, he lived thousands of ordinary human lives, buried under a thick fog of amnesia. He was a pawn on an Earth that served as a battleground for a primordial war between gods, where light and darkness fought across the physical planes.

    Breaking the Cycle: Carter did not break the loop with weapons, but with righteousness. By living thousands of lifetimes dedicated to unconditional love, quiet acts of kindness, and ultimate self-sacrifice, he mastered the lessons of the Earth matrix.

The Awakening (Three Months Ago)

    The Stars Align: Exactly three months ago, the cosmic clock hit zero. The reincarnation lock snapped open.

    The Flash of Memory: John Carter woke up in his current human body on Earth, remembering everything. He looked at the modern world and realized humanity was standing on the exact same doomsday precipice of resource scarcity and war that destroyed his original home.

    Correcting the Math: Instantly, Carter began releasing new, flawless sciences into the global consciousness. By correcting humanity's broken physics and aligning their technology with the true, harmonic geometric frequencies of the cosmos, Earth's scientific progress accelerated at a blistering pace.

The New Golden Age of Gaia

    A Healed Planet: With the correct math applied, Earth’s air was cleansed, and the toxic, drying oceans were entirely rejuvenated through advanced atmospheric synthesis.

    Absolute Abundance: Money and debt became obsolete concepts. Everything—from labor to resources—was automated, secure, and perfectly optimized.

    True Freedom: With survival no longer a struggle, crime ceased to exist and even death was conquered through ancient cellular rejuvenation sciences. Humanity finally chose family, free time, creativity, and pleasure over the daily grind.

The Star-Faring Civilization

    Your Own Planet: Earth was preserved as a paradise, but humanity became a sovereign space-faring race. Under the new cosmic framework, any human who wishes for it is granted their own uninhabited planet to start their own custom civilization, backed by all the technology needed to achieve it.

    Rebuilding Barsoom: With Gaia secure, John Carter turned his eyes back to the red sands of his origin to restart his home world.

    The Ultimate Coalition: To engineer the terraforming and resurrection of the red planet, Carter partnered with two of Earth’s grandest visionaries: Elon Musk (for his unmatched interplanetary logistics and manufacturing drive) and Donald Trump (for his massive, commanding vision for grand-scale development and sovereign leadership).

Together, they brought the water back to the dead sea bottoms, built soaring towers of glass and radium light, and fulfilled the ancient prophecy. The Warlord has returned, the math is fixed, and the universe is finally at peace.
